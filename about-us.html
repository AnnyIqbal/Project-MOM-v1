About Us

MOM - My Online Market
An E-Commerce website created to make online shopping fun for everyone.
It is fast, secure, reliable and user friendly. MOM supports both - the buyer and the seller at the same time.

If you are a BUYER,
Select a product of your choice among the numerous brands displayed on MOM.

If you are a SELLER,
Post an ad of your brand or product that will be visible to every buyer visiting MOM.

If you want to PLACE AN AD,
Post your sponsorship ad on MOM.

HOW TO BUY A PRODUCT ?

Buying a product on MOM is as easy as 1, 2 and 3.

  1. Just click on your favourite product to view its details.
  2. Hit the Buy button and mention your address where you want your order to be delivered.
  3. Submit your payment details.

HOW TO SELL A PRODUCT ?

Selling your product is a matter of buyer's choice! It'll be sold the second a buyer wants it...
To sell your product, just display it on MOM.

HOW TO DISPLAY A PRODUCT TO BE SOLD ?

Displaying a product to be sold is as easy as 1, 2 and 3.

  1. Click on the Sell button to navigate yourself to the SELL page.
  2. Place a picture of your product with its details.
  3. Hit the submit button.

HOW TO PLACE AN AD ?

Placing an ad on MOM is as simple as 1, 2 and 3.

  1. Just enter your name & email.
  2. Upload your ad file.
  3. Give description and Submit.
